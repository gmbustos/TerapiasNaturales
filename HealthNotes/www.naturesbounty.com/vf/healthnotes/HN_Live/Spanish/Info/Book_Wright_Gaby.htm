The Patient’s Book of Natural Healing

    The Patient’s Book of Natural Healing (Prima, 1999) by Jonathan V. Wright, MD, and Alan R. Gaby, MD

    The Patient’s Book of Natural Healing is a user-friendly reference guide to 50 of the most common health problems. The book presents information in an easy-to-read format. Each chapter recounts an office visit between Dr. Wright and one of his patients suffering from the condition in question, followed by Dr. Gaby’s commentary and review of supporting scientific literature. Written by two of the country’s most prominent natural health professionals, the book highlights the use of diet, nutritional supplements, herbs, hormones, and other natural substances in the prevention and treatment of common ailments, including arthritis, asthma, diabetes, high cholesterol, heart disease, infertility, migraines, and many more.
