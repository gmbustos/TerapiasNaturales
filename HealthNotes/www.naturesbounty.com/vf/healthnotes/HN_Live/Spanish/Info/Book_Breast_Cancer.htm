Breast Cancer: What You Should Know

    Breast Cancer: What You Should Know (But May Not Be Told) About Prevention, Diagnosis, and Treatment (Prima, 1994) by Steve Austin, ND, and Cathy Hitchcock, MSW

    This is considered the primary book written for women with breast cancer that covers what is known about the use of natural medicine. This book also covers the basics of conventional diagnosis and treatment, explaining the pros and cons of various diagnostic and treatment possibilities from a neutral position in order to help patients make decisions from a place of knowledge rather than ignorance.
