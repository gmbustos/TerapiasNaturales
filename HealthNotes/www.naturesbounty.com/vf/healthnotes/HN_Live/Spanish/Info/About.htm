About Healthnotes, Inc.

    About Healthnotes

    Healthnotes, Inc. (HNI), is the premier provider of credible, easy-to-use health and lifestyle information. Incorporating research gathered from scientific studies published in over 550 medical and scientific journals, Healthnotes has created a suite of applications, including Healthnotes Healthy Living, Healthnotes Pharmacy, Foodnotes, Fresh Produce, and Meat & Seafood, as well as other online products such as Healthnotes Newswire, Clinical Essentials, and Quick!Reference.

    Now in its seventh edition, the Healthnotes suite of applications provides consumers with practical, comprehensive information on health, wellness, food, and nutrition at the point of decision.

    Healthnotes is available through in-store, touchscreen kiosks in over 6,500 pharmacies, supermarkets, and natural food stores in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. It is licensed to Internet consumer and e-commerce sites, health management organizations, and manufacturers.

    Development and maintenance of Healthnotes content is overseen by Alan R. Gaby, MD, Chief Medical Editor.

    The HNI Executive team has extensive experience in medical publishing, education, retail, and technology.
